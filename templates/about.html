About Get Better

Our Mission

Get Better is a non-profit initiative dedicated to helping people quit harmful substances and improve their lives. We believe that change is possible for everyone, and with the right information and support, anyone can build a healthier future.

Why We Exist

Most resources about quitting harmful substances are either too clinical or too motivational (corny). We wanted something different—something real, honest, and actionable. We provide:

  • In-depth information: Not just "this is bad for you" but detailed explanations of exactly why and how it damages your body
  • Personalized plans: Everyone is different. Your plan accounts for your frequency of use, lifestyle, and goals
  • Multi-goal support: Want to quit vaping AND get in shape? Do both at the same time with our platform
  • Science-backed advice: Everything is based on research, not myths or trends
  • Non-corny tone: We talk to you like an adult, not with motivational posters and clichés

How It Works

  1. Sign up for free - no credit card required
  2. Answer questions about what you need help with (substances, lifestyle, looks, or combinations)
  3. Provide details like frequency of use, current fitness level, or personal goals
  4. Get your plan - personalized, actionable, and science-backed recommendations
